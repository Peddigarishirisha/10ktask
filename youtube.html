abstract

Directed by Gautham Menon, 'Vaaranam Aayiram' aka 'Surya Son of Krishnan'features Suriya in a dual role as father and son,.the film is about the life journeys of Suriya's both characters Simran, Sameera Reddy, and Divya Spandana play female leads, and the film has music composed by Harris Jayaraj.
